Game of the Week: Adventure

Adventure

This absolute classic game is showing up on our "Game of the Week" to remind us where games all came from and where they're going; that and I've been playing this again recently (streaming from 7pm-11pm every Tuesday and Thursday). Between this being the one of the very first open roam worlds to being the actual first game to ever have an easter egg, the creators name in a secret room, you can easily suck you in for hours of great gameplay!

While the graphics might not be appealing at first, this is a game you just have to pick up to truly understand.
