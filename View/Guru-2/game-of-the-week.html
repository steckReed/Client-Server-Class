Game of the Week: Minecraft

Minecraft

The ability to create anything you want combined with the vast amount of resources provided allows any person, young and old, to step foot in a game of endless possibilities. From building, mining, crafting, farming to destroying towns and killing mobs with, or without friends, Minecraft in my opinion is forever a timeless classic thats easy to pick up, and even harder to put down.

This is an absolute must have game and the reason that this beauty is my game of the week!
